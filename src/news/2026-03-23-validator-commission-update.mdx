## what changed

- **dot01** (polkadot): commission adjusted to **13.1415%** (a nod to pi day, since the issuance reduction landed on march 14)
- **ksm02, ksm03** (kusama): commission adjusted to 20%
- **ksm01** (kusama): retiring from active set

## what we're funding

we run more than validators. [dotters.network](https://dotters.network) provides free public rpc endpoints for asset hub and other polkadot parachains. on top of that, we're actively building open source tools on asset hub:

- [sonotxt.com](https://sonotxt.com) — ai marketplace on pallet-revive
- [syndigate.org](https://syndigate.org) — organization registry with wyoming dao llc filing
- [whodb.org](https://whodb.org) — identity search engine
- [w3registrar](https://github.com/rotkonetworks/w3registrar) — identity registrar

the updated commission helps sustain both validation and the public infrastructure we provide to the network.

## the numbers

| | monthly commission income | monthly infra cost |
|---|---|---|
| dot01 (13.14%) | ~2,500 DOT (~$3,500) | ~$300 (2U + ~80mbit/s) |
| ksm02 (20%) | ~21 KSM (~$85) | ~$300 (2U + ~80mbit/s) |
| ksm03 (20%) | ~21 KSM (~$85) | ~$300 (2U + ~80mbit/s) |
| rpc infra | $0 (unpaid) | ~$200 (servers + bandwidth) |

polkadot validator commission currently subsidizes kusama operations and rpc infrastructure. with the upcoming dap phase 2 (q2-q3 2026), validators will receive a proposed $2,000/month stablecoin operational reward plus vested dot rewards proportional to self-stake — properly compensating infrastructure operators going forward.

## ksm01 retiring

ksm01 is no longer in the kusama active set. nominators on ksm01 should move to **ksm02** or **ksm03**.

## good things coming for nominators

starting end of march 2026 (runtime 2.1.1):

- 10% minimum commission network-wide (we're already above this)
- 10,000 DOT minimum validator self-stake (we meet this)
- session key management moves to asset hub (~60 DOT deposit)

and for nominators (expected q2 2026):

- nominators become **unslashable** — validators absorb all slashing risk
- unbonding drops from 28 days to **24-48 hours**

## summary

- polkadot nominators: commission is now 13.1415%
- kusama nominators on ksm02/ksm03: commission is now 20%
- kusama nominators on ksm01: please move nominations to ksm02 or ksm03
- coming soon: zero slashing risk and faster unbonding for all nominators
