## what's new

We've opened our Bangkok infrastructure to customers. Three ways to put workloads on
our owned bare metal and AS142108, with no cloud middlemen and no markup.

- **VM hosting.** KVM virtual machines on PCIe 4.0 NVMe, configured to the exact spec
  you need. Dial in cores down to a fraction (true micro-VMs), the RAM, NVMe and traffic
  you want, and pay only for what you allocate. IPv6-first, IPv4 opt-in.
- **Bare metal.** Rent a dedicated Ryzen server, single-tenant, on the same routed BGP
  fabric, with KVM/IPMI console. 1 to 3 day delivery in Asia.
- **Colocation.** Bring your own hardware into our rack with direct BGP, redundant
  power, and your own IPv4/IPv6 space.

## why it's different

- **Owned AS, owned silicon.** We announce our own IPv6 /48s on AS142108 and run our own
  EPYC and Ryzen hardware. You pay real cost, not platform rent.
- **In-region.** Sub-30ms across Bangkok, Jakarta, KL and Hong Kong, on metal that's
  physically in Thailand, not routed through Singapore.
