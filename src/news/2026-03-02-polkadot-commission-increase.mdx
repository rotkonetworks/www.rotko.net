## what changed

we raised commission on our polkadot validator **dot01** from 0.9% to 5%, effective today.

## why

polkadot governance is moving toward a network-wide minimum commission of 10% as part of a [broader set of changes](https://forum.polkadot.network/t/changes-on-polkadot-in-march-2026/17101) landing after the march 12 runtime upgrade. rather than jump from 0.9% to 10% overnight, we're stepping up gradually so nominators have time to adjust expectations.

next increase to 10% will happen when the runtime enforces it (expected 1-2 weeks after the upgrade).

## what's coming for nominators

the same set of changes introduces major improvements for nominators:

- **no more slashing** — once validators meet the new minimum self-stake of 10,000 DOT, nominators become unslashable. validator operators absorb all slashing risk.
- **faster unbonding** — unbonding period drops from 28 days to 24-48 hours (expected april 2026)

higher commission is the tradeoff for validators taking on all the slashing risk. we think that's a good deal for nominators.

## what this means for you

- your staking rewards decrease slightly (5% commission instead of 0.9%)
- no action needed on your side
- expect another increase to 10% when the network minimum takes effect
- in return: zero slashing risk and much faster unbonding are coming
